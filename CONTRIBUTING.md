# Contributing to Cthulhu Forever

Thank you for your interest in making **Cthulhu Forever** better! This is a fan-made project and community feedback is what keeps it improving. Here's how you can help.

## 🧪 Test and Report

The best way to contribute is simply to **use the tool** and tell us what you find:

- Try generating characters across different eras (Victorian, Jazz Age, Cold War, Modern, and others).
- Push edge cases — unusual archetypes, typed skills, bond configurations, and export/import round-trips.
- Note anything that looks wrong, feels off, or could be clearer.

## 🐛 Raise Issues on GitHub

If you find a bug or have a feature suggestion, please open an issue on GitHub:

👉 [github.com/jonassode/cthulhu-forever/issues](https://github.com/jonassode/cthulhu-forever/issues)

**Good issues include:**

1. A clear title that summarises the problem.
2. Steps to reproduce the issue (what you did, what you expected, what actually happened).
3. Screenshots or screen recordings wherever possible — a picture is worth a thousand words.
4. The era, archetype, and any relevant character choices that triggered the problem.

## 💬 Comment on Itch.io

You can also leave feedback, share your experience, or post suggestions directly on the Itch.io project page:

👉 [Cthulhu Forever on Itch.io](https://jonassoderstrom.itch.io/cthulhu-forever)

Community comments there help others discover the project and give the maintainer a sense of how the tool is being used in the wild.

## 📋 Guidelines for All Feedback

- **Be specific.** Vague reports are hard to act on. The more detail you provide, the faster something can be fixed.
- **Include screenshots.** Especially for layout or rendering issues, a screenshot (or comparison with what you expected) makes diagnosis much faster.
- **Be kind.** This is a passion project maintained in spare time. Constructive, respectful feedback is always welcome.

## 🙏 Thank You

Every bug report, suggestion, and kind word makes this project better. Thank you for taking the time to help!
